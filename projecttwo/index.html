    • Our Story

    Jackpot Records began in 1997 in Portland, Oregon's Hawthorne neighborhood as an independent retailer set on getting music into the hands and ears of the people who love it. Jackpot Records is founded on the idea that the music matters, offering a wide selection of both New and Used Vinyl Lp's and cd's.
